SANDBOX

Concave Studio

  • Web Developer
  • Motion Designer
  • Designer / Illurtator

    • "Sandbox" represents more than just a showcase; it's a journey back to the carefree days of our childhood, where the sandbox was our canvas for boundless imagination. This theme invites us to rediscover the pure joy of creativity, where every grain of sand holds the potential for something extraordinary. Just as we once sculpted castles and forged adventures in the sandbox, this event celebrates the transformative power of imagination. Through the top capstones of De La Salle College of Saint Benilde, "Sandbox" becomes a vibrant tapestry of innovation, reminding us that the most profound ideas often originate from moments of playful exploration.

    2023

    BLOOM PROJECT

    Bloom Project Team

  • Web Developer
  • Designer / Illurtator

    • The "Bloom Project" is a compassionate initiative shedding light on the challenges faced by women with PCOS in the Philippines. Through a website and magazine, it offers a holistic perspective, acknowledging the emotional toll of PCOS alongside its physical effects. By fostering empathy and understanding, the project provides support and resources, empowering women to navigate their journey with resilience and grace. It's a beacon of solidarity and hope, celebrating the strength and beauty that can flourish amidst adversity.

    Freelance

    2019

    SGCHOW

    Freelance

  • Designer / Illurtator

    • "SGCHOW" is an engaging exhibit showcasing Singapore's rich and diverse food culture. Held at the FOOD and BEVERAGE EXPO 2019, visitors were treated to a tantalizing journey through Singapore's culinary landscape. From traditional hawker fare to modern gastronomic delights, "SGCHOW" celebrated the vibrant flavors and unique dining experiences that define Singaporean cuisine. Attendees had the opportunity to explore the intricate blend of cultures and influences that make Singapore a true foodie paradise.
